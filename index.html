In today's digital age, the process of digitalizing things has become increasingly important and prevalent. Digitalization involves transforming analog information into digital format, making it easily accessible, replicable, and shareable. This has revolutionized various industries and sectors, including communication, entertainment, education, and healthcare. We all use technology everyday whether it is phones, laptops, or tablets, there are many useful ways we can use technolgy to benefit or harm our community. So, these following articles talk about the different uses of digitization.

As a result of this project, my team has learned that computer science has both positive and negative impacts on our society. From DNA testing to AI-generated actors, computer science has proven to be both amazing and impactful.
